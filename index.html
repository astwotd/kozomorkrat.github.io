I collect forgotten fragments - driftwood, old fabric, bark, dry herbs, bones - to breathe new life into them, turning them into artifacts of memory. My work exists between forest and home, between myth and reality, carrying the spirit of Slavic folklore, pagan rituals, and ancestral traditions.

These are not just objects - they are totems, talismans, protective symbols infused with stories of forgotten grandmothers and grandfathers, whispers of the forest, traces of old rituals. I am inspired by Slavic mythology, tales of witches, wise elders, forest spirits, and ancient sorcery passed down through generations.
